About Us

Jakarta Open Trip & Local Experience

Who We Are

Jakarta Unlocked is a local travel platform dedicated to providing unique and immersive experiences throughout Jakarta. Founded by passionate travelers and local guides, our goal is to go beyond the typical tourist attractions and introduce visitors to the city's hidden gems, cultural heritage, culinary hotspots, and vibrant communities. Every tour is carefully designed to offer authentic experiences while supporting local businesses and promoting responsible tourism.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make exploring Jakarta easy, enjoyable, and meaningful by connecting travelers with knowledgeable local guides and carefully curated experiences. We strive to showcase the city's diversity while creating unforgettable memories for every visitor.

Our Vision

To become Jakarta's leading local experience platform, inspiring both domestic and international travelers to discover the city's unique culture, history, and lifestyle through authentic and sustainable tourism.

Why Choose Us

  • Curated local experiences beyond the typical tourist attractions.
  • Friendly and knowledgeable local guides.
  • Small group tours for a more personal experience.
  • Affordable packages with transparent pricing.
  • Safe, comfortable, and memorable adventures.
  • Easy online booking with responsive customer support.

We believe the best way to discover Jakarta is through authentic local experiences. By combining knowledgeable guides, carefully planned itineraries, and small-group adventures, we help every traveler explore the city's culture, history, and hidden gems with confidence and ease.

Our Promise

At Jakarta Unlocked, we believe every journey should be more than just visiting places—it should be about creating meaningful connections and lasting memories. We are committed to providing authentic experiences, exceptional service, and unforgettable adventures that allow you to see Jakarta from a local's perspective. Whether you're exploring historic streets, tasting legendary street food, discovering creative neighborhoods, or enjoying Jakarta's vibrant nightlife, we'll make sure every trip is enjoyable, safe, and worth remembering.
